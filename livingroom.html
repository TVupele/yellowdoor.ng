Private Room

Our exclusive Private Room offers an intimate setting for special occasions, business meetings, or private gatherings. With capacity for up to 20 guests, this elegantly appointed space features custom lighting, premium sound system, and personalized service.

Enjoy a curated menu prepared by our executive chef, with wine pairing options available. The room can be configured to suit your event needs, from boardroom-style meetings to celebratory dinners.

Wings & Tails Night

Every Friday night, our Living Room transforms into Abuja's premier social destination with our famous Wings & Tails event. Enjoy our signature chicken wings with a selection of premium cocktails and spirits.

Featuring live DJ performances and a vibrant atmosphere, it's the perfect way to kick off your weekend. Reservations are recommended as this popular event often sells out.

Event & Restaurant Hall

Our versatile Event Hall can accommodate up to 50 guests for weddings, corporate events, and social gatherings. The space features state-of-the-art AV equipment, customizable lighting, and a dedicated event planning team.

During regular hours, the hall serves as our main restaurant area, offering a sophisticated dining experience with panoramic views of the city.

Conference Room

Our professional conference room is equipped with modern technology for productive business meetings and presentations. The space can accommodate up to 30 guests in theater-style seating or 15 in boardroom setup.

We provide all necessary amenities including high-speed WiFi, projection equipment, flip charts, and premium catering services to ensure your business event runs smoothly.

Why Choose Our Spaces

Our Living Room and event spaces are designed to provide exceptional experiences for every guest. From the carefully curated ambiance to the attentive service, every element is thoughtfully considered to ensure your comfort and satisfaction.

Capacity ranges from intimate 10-person gatherings in our Private Room to 150-guest events in our main hall. Our professional team can assist with all aspects of your booking, including menu selection, decor, and technical requirements.
