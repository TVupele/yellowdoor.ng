Our Unique Spaces

Private Room

Our exclusive Private Room offers an intimate setting for special occasions, business meetings, or private gatherings. With capacity for up to 20 guests, this elegantly appointed space features custom lighting, premium sound system, and personalized service.

Enjoy a curated menu prepared by our executive chef, with wine pairing options available. The room can be configured to suit your event needs, from boardroom-style meetings to celebratory dinners.

Guest Parking Space

Enjoy stress-free stays with our secure, well-lit parking lot—conveniently located and monitored for your peace of mind. No hassle, just seamless access to your YellowDoor experience.

Event & Restaurant Hall

Our event space is ideal for birthdays, anniversaries, office training, and weddings. It features a flexible layout, elegant décor, and an outdoor area. We offer catering, bar services, and dedicated staff for a seamless experience. The space accommodates up to 50 seated guests or 80 for cocktail-style gatherings. Advance booking (7 days minimum) is required, with custom packages available."

Conference Room

Our Conference Room is perfect for meetings, brainstorming, and remote work, offering a quiet and professional setting. Amenities include high-speed Wi-Fi, power outlets, comfortable seating, a projector, and a whiteboard. Open daily to Walk-ins but reservations are recommended for private use. Flexible rental options include hourly, half-day, and full-day bookings.

Why Choose Our Spaces

Our Living Room and event spaces are designed to provide exceptional experiences for every guest. From the carefully curated ambiance to the attentive service, every element is thoughtfully considered to ensure your comfort and satisfaction.

Capacity ranges from intimate 10-person gatherings in our Private Room to 150-guest events in our main hall. Our professional team can assist with all aspects of your booking, including menu selection, decor, and technical requirements.

Flavors That Ignite the Senses

From crispy, saucy wings to rich, aromatic native dishes, every bite at The Living Room is a celebration of bold flavors. Dive into a world of perfectly grilled seafood, vibrant salads, and indulgent desserts—each dish crafted to delight. This is where great food, great vibes, and unforgettable moments come together.
